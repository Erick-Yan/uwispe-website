CANADA'S 1ST ESTABLISHED STUDENT CHAPTER.

THE NEXT GENERATION.

As Canada's first student chapter, we are invested in inspiring and cultivating the next generation of students looking to change the pharmaceutical and biotech industry. We are a branch under the International Society for Pharmaceutical Engineering, an organization that is committed to the educational and technical advancement of its members while striving to provide solutions towards complex pharmaceutical industry challenges. We hope to create unique experiences for Waterloo students that will expose them to the essential knowledge and ideas that can push them towards success. In an industry that's often perceived to be outdated in its practices, we are looking to change that view through modern big data, machine learning, and equipment/facility design applications.

Industry Knowledge.

25.0%

Student Chapter members will be exposed to an endless number of professional resources provided by ISPE. The ISPE website platform holds many online courses, Guidance Documents, training modules, online forums, Mentorship Programs and magazines to help develop an understanding of various industry concepts and trends.

Experience.

50.0%

Our school will be holding pharmaceutical and biotech facility tours to expose students to the underlying work and decisions being made by professional engineers on a daily basis. We are also looking to facilitate yearly hackathons to push students to utilize their acquired knowledge and skills to tackle existing industry problems.

Social Connections.

75.0%

We will be bringing the industry to our members through professional engineer speakers that will come in to share their experiences and knowledge to Waterloo students. ISPE also holds global conferences throughout the year, assembling the best pharmaceutical professionals from all over the world to discuss present industry challenges and developments. These opportunities will provide the optimal setting for students to explore ways they could collaborate and work with professionals in the future.

Career Building.

100.0%

As a student chapter, our duty is to provide all the necessary tools and opportunities to help foster a successful career for each member. The culmination of the benefits stated prior will hopefully generate enough passion and proficiency to transition students into the working field through internship or full-time job opportunities.
